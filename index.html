Coleman & Associates Land Surveying

Quality since 1983

William M. Coleman, (RPLS, GISP) with over 40 years experience, opened Coleman & Assoc. Land Surveying in 1983. We provide quality professional surveying and mapping services including, but not limited to: construction staking, field design surveys, topographic surveys, boundary surveys and analysis, preparation of easement or right-of-way exhibits, GPS monumentation, and other professional survey services to public and private enterprise. Our well-trained field crews and technicians work under the guidance of an in-house RPLS to bring you an efficiently executed, extraordinary product.
